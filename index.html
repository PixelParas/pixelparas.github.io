Important

1. Download mods setup
2. Extract it and run setup_mods.bat

To Enable Shaders

Open minecraft go to Options->Video Settings->ShaderPacks and enable BSL_v8.1.02.1 / Complementry (some dont work with Immersive portals)

For Connected Textures

Go to Options->Resource Packs and click on both the continuity packs to add them to right side

Changing Physics Mod Settings

Go to Options->Physics Settings-> Set Block Physics to Particles instead of Fractured
